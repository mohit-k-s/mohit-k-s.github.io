Mohit’s Blog

Hi, I’m Mohit.

I spend most of my time working on backend systems — databases, distributed architecture, scaling things that break, and thinking about how things talk to each other. I’m drawn to the edge cases. The weird bugs. The deep internals.

I write to make sense of the world, one entry at a time — sometimes about tech, sometimes about cities I’ve walked through, and sometimes about the strange quiet between thoughts.

This blog is a map of what I’m learning, what I’m building, and what I’m trying to figure out.
